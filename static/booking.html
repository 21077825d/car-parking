Disney Public Car Park Information

Car parks for Hong Kong Disneyland Park are available for private cars, coaches, and motorcycles.

The capacity of the car park includes approximately 1,400 parking spaces for private cars, 240 spaces for coaches, and 40 spaces for motorcycles.

For your convenience, there is a designated drop-off/pick-up area beside the car park.
